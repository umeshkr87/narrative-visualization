Meat Consumption Trend of the World

By Kumar, Umesh <umesh2@illinois.edu>

Graph: Per Capita Meat Consumption over the time


Here chart shows per capita meat consumption (excluding seafood and fish ) per year of the world. It shows trend in per capita meat consumtion of World and few notable countries such as United States, China and Gambia. On average world's meat consumption has increased from 22 kg in 60's to 43 kg in 2020. Individual country such as China which underwent a massive economic growth, consumes per capita 15X more meat since 1961. High-income countries such as United States has seen slower growth in meat consumtion. United States had 111 kg in 1970 and 116 kg in 2015 which shows much slower growth compared to China.
